Mission

We are a group of PHP developers who use VIM every day or not.

First we would like to share with other phpVimmers: tips, plugins, experience and all other breakages caused by VIM.

In addition vim-php is a Github organization that aims to collect PHP related Vim plugins.
You have a plugin? Share it with us to be helped by the community!
